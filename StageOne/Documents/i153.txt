Stars gear up for Bafta ceremony

Film stars from across the globe are preparing to walk the red carpet at this year's Bafta award ceremony.

The 2005 Orange British Academy Film Awards are being held at The Odeon in London's Leicester Square. A host of Hollywood stars, including <person> Cate Blanchett </> , <person> Leonardo DiCaprio </> , <person> Keanu Reeves </>  and <person> Richard Gere </> , are expected to attend Saturday's ceremony. Hosted by <person> Stephen Fry </> , the glittering ceremony will be broadcast on BBC One at 2010 GMT.
